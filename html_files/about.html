Mission Statement

Here at Celebrity Beefs, we strive to show the world that fighting amongst celebrities really isn't something to be interested in. There are many other things that you can be doing with your time other than by reading/watching some celebrities fight over something someone said or did that is insignificant in the long run. We strive to help you bring actual joy in your life through various methods other than that we present here.

Core Values

Here at Celebrity Beefs, we strive to foster a culture of inclusion by showing what true respect and love looks like. We strive show everyone dignity and not getting overly frustrated by other's decisions. We recognized that we have our agency which meanse we choose how to act in any situation.
